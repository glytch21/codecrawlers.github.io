CodeCrawlers Gameplay Documentation

Welcome, Code Defenders, to the thrilling world of CodeCrawlers! This documentation is your guide to mastering the art of tower defense through coding. Prepare to defend your base against relentless viruses, harnessing the power of programming to secure victory.

Objective

Defend your base from waves of viruses by strategically placing and coding defense towers.

Getting Started

As you embark on your adventure in CodeCrawlers, you'll have the opportunity to select from different modes. However, please note that Story Mode becomes available only after completing Tutorial Mode, and Endless Mode unlocks once you've completed Story Mode.

Tutorial Mode:

New to coding or need a refresher? Tutorial Mode is the perfect place to start. Here, you'll learn the basics of programming as it applies to tower defense gameplay. Follow along with interactive lessons that guide you through fundamental coding concepts, tower placement strategies, and defense tactics.

Story Mode:

Ready to put your newfound knowledge to the test? Story Mode takes you on a journey where you'll apply the skills you've learned in Tutorial Mode. Navigate through challenging levels, each with its own unique set of coding puzzles and strategic objectives. Experience an immersive storyline as you defend your base against waves of viruses, uncovering secrets and unlocking new abilities along the way.

Endless Mode:

Are you a coding master looking for the ultimate challenge? Endless Mode is where you'll face relentless waves of viruses, pushing your programming skills to their limits. Test your endurance and creativity as you defend your base against an ever-increasing onslaught of enemies. How long can you survive?

Coding Challenges

Encounter progressively complex coding challenges as you advance through levels. Hone your programming skills with tasks involving loops, conditional statements, variables, and functions.

Learning Path

Follow a structured learning path that introduces coding concepts gradually. Interactive tutorials and in-game tips provide guidance, ensuring a positive learning experience.
